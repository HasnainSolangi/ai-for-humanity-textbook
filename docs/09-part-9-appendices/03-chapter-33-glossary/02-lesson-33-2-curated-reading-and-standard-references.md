# Curated Reading and Standard References

To go deeper, consult the primary sources.

## Standards and Frameworks
*   **NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF):** The US gold standard for managing AI risk. [Link](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)
*   **ISO/IEC 42001:** The global standard for AI Management Systems.
*   **EU AI Act:** The full text of the European regulation.

## Seminal Papers
*   **"Attention Is All You Need" (Vaswani et al., 2017):** The paper that introduced the Transformer architecture.
*   **"On the Dangers of Stochastic Parrots" (Bender et al., 2021):** A critical look at the risks of Large Language Models.
*   **"Constitutional AI" (Anthropic, 2022):** Using AI to supervise AI.

## Books
*   **"Human Compatible"** by Stuart Russell.
*   **"Weapons of Math Destruction"** by Cathy O'Neil.
*   **"The Alignment Problem"** by Brian Christian.
